SF Sights

We LOVE San Francisco! While you're visiting, we suggest checking out the following restaurants and sights. You really could eat your way through the city...

Restaurants & Eateries

  • Plow - for really delicious brunch. If you're single, you might even meet your soulmate here! This is where we had our VERY FIRST date. (Portero Hill, $$)
  • The House - excellent Asian fusion dinner. (North Beach, $$)
  • Ha Nam Ninh - Delicious "dry pho". (Tenderloin, $)
  • Nick's Crispy Tacos - Get the pescado, Nick's way. (North Beach, $$)
  • Mensho SF - Quite possibly the best ramen in the US. Prepare for a wait! (Tendernob, $$)
  • Blue Barn - Healthy and delicious salads. (Russian Hill, Marina/Cow Hollow, $)
  • Philz Coffee - Get the Mint Mojito, Philz's signature drink. (TL, SoMA, $)
  • Blue Bottle Coffee - More coffee
  • Sightglass Coffee - More coffee
  • Boba Guys - Best place in town for boba. (Mission, Hayes Valley, $)
  • Mr. and Mrs. Miscellaneous - artisanal ice cream. (Dogpatch, $)
  • Bi-Rite Ice Cream - famous ice cream. (NoPa, Mission, $)
  • Loving Cup - rice pudding! (Hayes Valley, Russian Hill/North Beach, $)

Things to Do & Places to See

  • Golden Gate Park
  • Lovers Lane/Presidio
  • Fisherman's Wharf & Alcatraz Island (get tickets in advance!)
  • Dolores Park
  • Painted Ladies/Alamo Square
